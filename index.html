Data Deletion Policy

At DK Devs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. If you would like to request the deletion of your data associated with our Facebook app, please follow the steps below:

  1. Submit a Data Deletion Request: To request the deletion of your data, please contact our support team or fill out our Data Deletion Request Form.
  2. Verification: For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing your data deletion request. Please provide any requested information to help us confirm your identity.
  3. Data Deletion Timeline: We will process your data deletion request within 30 days from the date of your request.
  4. Confirmation: Once your data deletion request has been processed and your data has been deleted, we will notify you via email.

Please note that certain types of data may be retained for legal or compliance reasons, or data deletion may not be feasible in certain circumstances.

If you have any questions or concerns about the deletion of your data, please contact us.

Thank you for trusting us with your data.
